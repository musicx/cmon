## High-Signal Triggers

- Non-trivial root cause discovered: <yes | no>
- Reusable guardrail or invariant established: <yes | no>
- Prior assumption or pattern was corrected: <yes | no>
- Operational gotcha or rollout constraint surfaced: <yes | no>
- Existing solution doc is now stale or contradicted: <yes | no>

## Medium-Signal Triggers

- Review surfaced a reusable acceptance heuristic: <yes | no>
- The work created a pattern likely to recur in the same area: <yes | no>
- Future planning would be meaningfully better with this lesson documented: <yes | no>

## Low-Signal Or Non-Triggers

- Trivial typo or wording fix only: <yes | no>
- One-off local cleanup with no reusable lesson: <yes | no>
- Restatement of an already-documented rule with no new evidence: <yes | no>
